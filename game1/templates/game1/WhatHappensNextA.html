Before you compete against two other people, we give you a choice.

You may choose to compete in an environment where the score of each of the three players in Part 1 is shown to you before you start the competition. We refer to this environment as Firm A.

Alternatively, you may choose to compete in an environment where you know only your own score in Part 1 and do not know the scores of the other two players in Part 1. We refer to this environment as Firm B.

Summary: Firm A provides an environment where you compete after learning about your competitors' performance in Part 1.
Firm B provides an environment where you only know your own performance from Part 1.

Please think carefully about each environment and how it might affect your performance. When you feel confident that you understood these instructions, please proceed.
